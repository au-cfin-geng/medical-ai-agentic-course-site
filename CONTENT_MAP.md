## Notes on Content Status

All pages listed above were created as part of the initial site build and are marked "Complete." This means they contain substantive content that fulfils their described purpose — not that they cannot be improved. See `TODO_NEXT.md` for a prioritised list of improvements.

Pages most likely to benefit from early update:
- `docs/mri/t1_t2_flair.md` — benefits from annotated image examples (currently text-only)
- `docs/medical_ai_workflow/study_design.md` — a sample completed study design would greatly improve usability
- `docs/handouts/glossary.md` — terms will need to be expanded as student questions reveal gaps
- `docs/lab_missions/mission_03_investigate_failure.md` — could benefit from example failure case images embedded in the page
- `docs/prompt_library/bonus_prompts.md` — designed to grow as instructors contribute prompts they found effective
